About Our Hospital

The Federal Neuro-Psychiatric Hospital, Kaduna is a 550-bed hospital and a Federal Government owned psychiatric hospital. It is a center for postgraduate training in Psychiatry and has produced numerous Fellows of both the National and West African Postgraduate Medical Colleges.

Our mission is to provide evidence-based, affordable, and accessible mental health services, train a skilled mental health workforce, and conduct impactful research.
